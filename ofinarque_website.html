About Us

Ofinarque is a visionary architecture firm dedicated to creating innovative and sustainable designs. With our expertise and passion, we transform ideas into inspiring spaces that enrich lives and communities.

Paulo Jorge Contente, Lead Architect

With over 20 years of experience in sustainable and innovative design, Maria Rodriguez leads Ofinarque with vision and expertise. Her work has been recognized with numerous awards, including the prestigious Green Building Excellence Award in 2022.

Maria's portfolio spans a wide range of projects, from cutting-edge corporate headquarters to sustainable urban developments. Her unique approach combines aesthetic excellence with environmental responsibility, pushing the boundaries of modern architecture.

Education: M.Arch, Harvard Graduate School of Design
Notable Projects: EcoTower (New York), Harmony Plaza (Singapore), Urban Oasis (Barcelona)
